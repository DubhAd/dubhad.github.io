People keep asking what all the possible states that their entity could be. The answer is complicated.

For things other than sensor you can tell. For sensor you can't, it could be anything:

binary_sensor: on or off
switch: on or off
light: on or off
input_boolean: on or off
input_select: whatever you configured it with
device_tracker: home, not_home, or the name of a zone
media_player:
input_text:
input_number:
timer:
counter:
